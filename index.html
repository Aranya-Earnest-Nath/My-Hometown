Welcome to Maijdee

Maijdee is a charming town located in the heart of Noakhali. Known for its calm atmosphere, friendly people and cultural heritage, Maizdee offers a peaceful lifestle away from the noise of big cities. The town is home to important educational institutions and has a growing urban center, making it a blend of tradition and modernity.

Places to visit

  • Maijdee Jame Mosque
  • Noakhali Supermarket
  • Noakhali Science & Technology University

Famous Foods

"Panta Ilish" a traditional dish of fermented rice soaked in water, served with fresh Hilsa fish (Ilish). It's a beloved Bengali delicacy especially popular in Noakhali during the monsoon season and special occasions.
